What is KFL?

Kids Footy League

Kids version of the original AFL fantasy League

Aimed at 5-12 year old Children

Engage more children in the AFL select your players, follow their matches and compete with other kids to reach the top spot on the leaderboard.

How do I play?

Register on the portal with the help of your parents

Create your profile

Buy 5 players with the virtual currency of $100 from the current AFL season

Select your STAR player. His performance will fetch you double points

Follow the matches through the weekend

See your rank on the leaderboard every Monday

You can also Swap one player each week

What do I Get?

End of the season the top three kids on the leaderboard will get AFL merchandise, free match ticket for AFL grand final and chance to meet their favorite player.

Further top 10 kids on the leaderboard get a free training session from the official AFL training squad!
