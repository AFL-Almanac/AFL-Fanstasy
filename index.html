What is KFL?

Kid's Footy League

Kids version of the original AFL Fantasy League.

Aimed at 5-12 year old children.

The objective is to engage more children in the AFL.

Kid's can select their favorite players, follow AFL matches and compete with other kids to reach the top spot on the leaderboard.

How do I play?

Register on the portal with the help of your parents.

Create your profile.

Buy 5 players with the virtual currency of $1000 from the current AFL season.

Select your STAR player. His performance will fetch you double points.

Follow the matches through the weekend.

See your rank on the leaderboard every Monday.

You can also swap one player each week.

What do I get?

End of the season the top three kids on the leaderboard will get AFL merchandise and free match tickets for the AFL grand final and chance to meet their favorite player.

Further top 10 kids on the leaderboard get a free training session from the official AFL training squad!
